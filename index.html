Hello There!

I'm Sasi Mitra, a fifth-year BS-MS student at IISER Tirupati. I am currently completing my Master's thesis at the Australian National University under the supervision of Dr. Amit Seta, where I focus on the statistical properties of 3D Interstellar Medium (ISM) distributions.

Beyond astrophysics, I'm a developer who loves building practical web tools. This website serves as a portfolio of my research, a platform for my thoughts on science and tech, and a gallery for the random—but hopefully useful—projects I build.
